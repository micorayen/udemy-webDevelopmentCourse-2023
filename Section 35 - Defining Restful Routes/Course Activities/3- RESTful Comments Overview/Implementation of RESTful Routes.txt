NAME 	| PATH 			| VERB 		| PURPOSE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Index 	| /comments 		| GET		| Display all comments
New 	| /comments/new 		| GET 		| Form to create new comment

Delete 	| /comments/:id 		| DELETE		| Deletes specific item on server
