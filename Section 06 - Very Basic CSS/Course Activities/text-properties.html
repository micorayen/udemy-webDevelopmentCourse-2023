Origin

Colegio De San Gabriel Arcangel is an insttution that has been established way back January 1993 by the passion of Dr. Gabriel G. Uriarte to help his constituents of Sapang Palay to achieve their dreams of becoming professionals to lessen the burden of those hard times of living and turn the place more prosperous and successful. With the help and inspiration of his wife, Dr. Lucina P. Uriarte, it became possible through its step by step progress and it is committed since then to give quality education that molds one’s future.

History

Since 1993, Colegio de San Gabriel Arcangel started its way on contributing quality education that produces competitive individuals to conquer global realities. The school had an initial number of 197 enrollees for complete preschool and grades 1 to 5 on School Year 1993-1994. The succeeding years School Year 1994-1995, they have completed the curriculum for the preschool, elementary and high school with the total of 550 students.

Courses Information

On the year 2002 – 2003, the school finally started operating tertiary level by offering TESDA courses. CDSGA is currently offering TESDA courses like Bread and Pastry NC II, Food and Beverage Services NC II, Hotel and Restaurant Services, Housekeeping NC II, Associate in Bookkeeping and Accounting, Caregiving NC II, Diploma in Midwifery, Diploma in Practical Nursing, Health Care Services NC II, Associate in Computer Science, Associate in Information
